Collective Action for Water Manangement

The current collective action work builds on 4 years of ongoing efforts towards a sustainable Aconcagua Valley

Aconcagua is an important source for fruits like avocados – however, supply is under threat due to an imbalance between water supply and water demand.

Find out more

Why the Aconcagua Valley?

Important Agriculture

The Aconcagua valley in has a long history of agriculture and is a high-quality supplier of avocados, citrus, cherries, and other fruits for the UK and EU​​.

Read More

Shortage Risks

The current water withdrawals in the Aconcagua valley are 35% higher than what can be renewed, and water availability will reduce by 50% in the next 25 years​. ​

Read More

Collaborative Action

Collaborative Action offers a solution. It allows buyers to contribute to sustainable farming, whilst tackling the complex challenges in the enabling environment and supply chains.​
